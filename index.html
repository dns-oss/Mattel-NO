3. Channel Strategy Optimization

Focus on key partners for stability and growth:

  • **computersalg.no:** Strongest distribution + 70 price wins. Ideal for leveraging scale.
  • **eurotoys.net:** Specialized toy retailer. Ideal for category expertise and premium showcasing.
  • **Strategic Address:** Need to create a strategy to address international platform competition (eBay/Tradeinn) without sacrificing local partner relationships.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Pricing Governance

  • Implement **Minimum Advertised Price (MAP)** policies to curb extreme price variations.
  • Focus coordination on high-visibility and premium products.
  • Develop a mechanism to address international platform arbitrage.

3. Premium Portfolio Focus

  • Continue investment in collector/signature lines that command premium pricing.
  • Leverage strategic Disney partnerships for elevated market positioning.
  • Use premium products to elevate overall brand perception across the market.

2. Channel Partnership Strategy

  • Strengthen ties with top performers (computersalg.no, eurotoys.net).
  • Consider exclusive product launches with key Norwegian specialists.
  • Develop unique product mixes tailored for different channel types (e.g., mass-market vs. specialty).

4. Competitive Monitoring

  • Implement systematic monitoring of the entire 108+ retailer network.
  • Focus particularly on international platforms disrupting local pricing.
  • Track price leadership metrics weekly to identify emerging competitive threats.
